FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is a Crypto Airdrop?

A Crypto Airdrop is when a blockchain project or ICO distributes free tokens or coins to the crypto community. In order to Participate, the users often a times need to do some social tasks, such as following Twitter, Telegram or more.

Are there any other names for Airdrops?

Sometimes Crypto companies use other names for their special promotions. For example, “Bounty” which means that users have to follow tasks regularly to get tokens, which is still very similar to Airdrops. Other names with similar meanings are “Token Drops”, “Token Challange”, “Smart Drops”, and more. As we see it, each airdrop has its own rules and the name does not really matter, as the idea is similar.

How do I make money with Airdrops?

When you join Airdrops, usually you need to wait until the tokens are distributed. We suggest you follow https://www.airdroprating.io/feed/ (which we are partnered with), which you can get updated when coins/tokens are distributed to your wallet. If the tokens are exchangeable you can exchange to Bitcoin or Ethererum on exchanges. If not, you will need to wait until it happens. You can not expect to earn real money from each airdrop you join, however, the good airdrops can have its token price go up in the long run.
